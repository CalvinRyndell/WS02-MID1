KAFKA

About Kafka

A Stellaron Hunter that hails from the planet, Pteruges-V. She doesn't know the feeling of fear yet is an expert at inflicting it unto others.

Kafka in "A Dramatic Irony".

Kafka's characteristics

  1. Kafka is a manipulative sociopath who follows the path of the Nihility.
  2. She is fully determined in realizing Elio's "script" to achieve her goals, regardless of its consequences.
  3. She bears a stillness that is both alluring yet menacing to her victims.

This is Kafka and her fellow Stellaron Hunters during the Japella Rebellion.
